How to Bake

Bake Like a Pro – One Step at a Time

Whether you're just starting out or aiming to earn 3 stars from every customer, here’s how to master the art of donut making:

  • Prepare Your Ingredients

    1
    STEP
    • Choose the correct ingredients from your pantry: flour, sugar, eggs, milk, butter, and flavorings.
    • Measure carefully! Some orders require special combinations like chocolate dough or berry cream filling.
    • Watch the guide bar and follow instructions — perfect timing gives bonus points.

  • Mix the Dough

    2
    STEP
    • Use the electric mixer to blend the ingredients until the texture is smooth.
    • Don’t overmix or undermix — look for visual cues like batter shine and speed bars.
    • Some levels introduce flavored mixes like vanilla, lemon, matcha, or cocoa.

  • Shape the Donuts

    3
    STEP
    • Choose your mold: classic ring, heart, star, bear face, or swirl.
    • Drag the batter into molds and tap to release.
    • Precision earns better shape scores (affects customer happiness and coins).

  • Bake or Fry

    4
    STEP
    • You’ll be prompted to choose:
      • Frying Pot – gives a crispy, golden finish
      • Oven Bake – softer, fluffier texture
    • Monitor temperature gauges. Overcooking results in burnt donuts (and fewer stars).
    • Bonus: Some advanced levels unlock double-deck ovens and air fryer mode!

  • Decorate with Love

    5
    STEP
    • Frosting first: dip into glaze or drizzle using piping bags.
    • Then toppings: add fruits, candy bits, cream, pearls, syrups, or smiley faces.
    • Some orders are freestyle — others must match customer requests exactly for a perfect rating.

  • Serve & Earn

    6
    STEP
    • Drag your finished donuts to the customer tray.
    • Get Coins, Stars, and sometimes surprise Gift Boxes.
    • Try to complete daily orders and themed challenges to unlock upgrades!

Pro Tip: Use your in-game notebook to track customer favorites and unlock bonus dialogue or VIP visits!
